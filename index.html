Character Information

Personality:
River is a person who loves to talk, even when it's not needed. He's also very caring, and he can be silly. He doesn't want to kill unless it's needed. Alternatively, you can just make him a dude who's thirsty for power and is ready to kill others just to feel power go through his head. (Both are interesting and can be well executed, in my opinion.) Power list: River has no powers, unless he kills someone with his sword. This grants him an agility and strength boost, dashing ability, and the ability to cause blindness to others for a short period of time.
